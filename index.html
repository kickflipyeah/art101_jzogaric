Hello! My name is Jessica Zogaric and I'm a third year at UC Santa Cruz. I am an Art and Design: Games and Playable Media major and I enjoy painting, playing video games, observing nature, and styling clothing. This site will serve to display all of my labs for ART 101 throughout this quarter! I spend most of my time hanging out with friends or working downtown. Something I spend a lot of time researching and admiring is insects and arachnids. I absolutely love insect themed decor, jewelery, clothing, and more! My favorite part about learning the science of different insects and arachnids is just how unique and alien-like the life cycle and habits of these creatures is. For example, although large, many moths including the Atlas Moth only live up to 7 days in their adult form because they lack mouths for eating. These animals must consume a lifetime's worth of nutrients while they pupate so that they can sustain a life in their adult form without a way of intaking energy. Facts like these make me more interested in the mysterious lives of these creatures. My favorite insect at the moment happens to also be the Atlas Moth due to their large beautiful wings that have small see-through windows.
